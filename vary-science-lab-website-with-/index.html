What We Do

Pushing the boundaries of computational science

Quantum Simulation
Real-time quantum field simulations with unprecedented fidelity, enabling researchers to visualize particle interactions at the Planck scale.
Neural Rendering
AI-accelerated volumetric rendering pipelines that transform raw simulation data into intuitive, explorable 3D visualizations in real time.
Predictive Modeling
Machine learning models trained on 12M+ simulations, capable of predicting complex system behaviors before physical experiments are run.
Data Architecture
Petabyte-scale data infrastructure built for scientific workloads — ingesting, processing, and serving simulation results at wire speed.
Open Collaboration
Every tool, dataset, and publication is open source. We believe breakthrough science happens when knowledge flows freely between institutions.
Frontier Research
From dark matter candidates to protein folding dynamics — our team tackles the hardest problems at the intersection of physics and computation.
Research Areas

Three pillars of discovery

01
Topological Quantum Fields
Exploring emergent phenomena in condensed matter through large-scale tensor network simulations and topological invariant analysis.
02
Cosmological N-Body Dynamics
Modeling gravitational interactions across billions of particles to understand dark matter halos and large-scale structure formation.
03
Molecular Dynamics at Scale
GPU-accelerated molecular simulations for drug discovery, materials science, and understanding the machinery of life at atomic resolution.
